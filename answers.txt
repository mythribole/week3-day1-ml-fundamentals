What is the slope?
The slope is the coefficient of the feature. It shows how much the predicted marks change when the feature increases by one unit.

What is the intercept?
The intercept is the starting value of the prediction when all input features are zero.

What do they mean?
Slope represents the relationship between input features and marks. Intercept represents the baseline prediction.

What is MAE?
Mean Absolute Error is the average difference between actual values and predicted values. Lower MAE means better performance.

What is R² Score?
R² Score measures how well the model explains the variation in the data. A value closer to 1 indicates better performance.

Is your model performing well?
Yes. The model performs well because the predictions are close to the actual values and the R² Score is high.
